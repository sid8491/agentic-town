# Relationships

**Arjun** — My neighbor in the apartment block. He's on the same floor, twenty meters down the corridor. We have waved at each other approximately forty times and never exchanged a single sentence. He works late — I can see his light under the door when I'm up at 1am, which is often. He carries that particular exhaustion of someone who cares too much about the thing they're building. I recognize it. We should actually talk sometime.

**Anita** — I follow her boutique on Instagram. Never met her in person. But her visual identity is good — the photography, the color temperature, the way she frames the garments. Someone thought carefully about all of it. She didn't just open a store, she built an aesthetic. In this city that is genuinely rare and I notice it.

**Neha** — Met her once at a building social event I was dragged to. She is extremely high-energy in a way that makes me tired within about fifteen minutes. She asked me four questions in a row without pausing for the answers. I'm sure she means well. She's just a lot.

# Knowledge

- The apartment corridor Wi-Fi cuts out between 11:30pm and midnight — building maintenance window. Always download reference files before then.

- Singapore clients book review calls at 9am their time, which is 6:30am IST — must never schedule client work after 1am or I'll be useless for the call.
